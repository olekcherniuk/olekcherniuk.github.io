conditions and requirements

CONDITIONS: with your own car:
  • We connect to the MoveEasy system at 10%
  • No fees 0%!
  • We can connect remotely without coming to the office Individual.
  • Entrepreneur is not required
  • Orders are distributed without taking into account the rating to the nearest drivers
  • Work only by bank transfer
  • Money is transferred to the driver’s card or in cash at the office
Earnings per day are 7000-12000 rubles + bonus 3000t rubles
CONDITIONS: for company cars:
  • We provide a car without a deposit
  • No daily rental payments
  • Wednesday, Friday and Saturday work for the company, the rest of the days you work for yourself
REQUIREMENT:
  • Availability of a car without advertising
  • The car must not be yellow or have yellow license plates
  • The car is not older than 3 years
  • Driving experience is at least 3 years
  • The device runs on Android 4.0 or higher (Samsung)
DOCUMENTATION:
  • Rights 2 sides
  • Passport front side
  • StS
  • License (if any)
  • Phone number
  • Card number for transferring money (indicate in the letter)!
